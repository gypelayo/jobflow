JobFlow Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

JobFlow is a browser extension designed to help you track job applications. Your privacy is important to us.

Data Collection

JobFlow stores all data locally in your browser. We do not have access to your data. Data is stored using:

  • IndexedDB — Stores your job applications, notes, and pipeline data
  • Chrome Storage API — Stores extension settings and preferences

External Services

JobFlow may send data to external AI providers only when you explicitly request job extraction. The data sent includes:

  • The content of the job posting page you're currently viewing

Supported AI providers:

  • OpenAI (api.openai.com)
  • Anthropic (api.anthropic.com)
  • Google (generativelanguage.googleapis.com)
  • Perplexity (api.perplexity.ai)
  • Ollama (localhost:11434) — local, no data leaves your machine

We do not collect, store, or transmit your job data to any of our servers.

Permissions

JobFlow requests the following permissions:

  • <all_urls> — Required to read job posting content from any website for AI extraction
  • activeTab — Required to extract content from the current tab when you click the extension
  • storage — Required to save your settings and job data locally
  • tabs — Required to manage extension popup windows

Analytics

We use GoatCounter for anonymous, privacy-friendly analytics on our website. This tracks page visits and download counts only — no personal data is collected.

Data Retention

All your data remains in your browser. You can:

  • Export your data at any time using the Export feature in the dashboard
  • Clear all data by uninstalling the extension or using the Clear Data feature

Third-Party Services

JobFlow uses the following third-party libraries:

  • sql.js — SQLite compiled to WebAssembly for local database operations
  • Chart.js — For analytics visualizations
  • jsPDF — For PDF resume generation

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via GitHub Issues.
